The volume begins with Frodo Baggins discovering the One Ring in a hidden cache. This inciting incident sets the story in motion. The narrative follows Frodo‚Äôs reluctant acceptance of the task to carry the Ring to Mount Doom, transitioning from domestic life to duty. Key events include:

## Book 2: The Two Towers

The structure becomes more fragmented and geographically dispersed. The story splits between two primary arcs:

This volume culminates in a confrontation at the Black Gate of Mordor, where forces of good and evil face off in a final battle. The structure is tightly focused on resolving the central conflict, progressing from the fall of Minas Tirith to the destruction of the Ring.
